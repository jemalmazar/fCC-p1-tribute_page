Anthony Bourdain

1965 - 2018

A celebrity chef, author, and travel documentarian. Anthony Bourdain starred in programs that explored international culture, cuisine, and the human condition.

A Cook's Tour

2002 - 2003, 35 episodes

Bad-boy chef and author Anthony Bourdain goes off the beaten track in search of foods that are rare, highly esteemed, and sometimes downright dangerous. The show was an offshoot of a best-selling book Bourdain wrote in 2001.

No Reservations

2005 - 2012, 142 episodes

Bad-boy chef and best-selling author Anthony Bourdain hits both culinary hotspots and out-of-the-way gems in his global quest for the ultimate dining experience. Along the way, Bourdain offers his perspective on both the food and the local customs he comes across his travels.

The Layover

2011 - 2013, 20 episodes

The many fans of Anthony Bourdain know he doesn’t need reservations to get the most out of his travel adventures. While awaiting his next departure, the sharp-tongues host is winging it like never before, killing time with a purpose in cities throughout the U.S., Asia, and Europe. But he has only between 24 and 48 hours to unleash an unpredictable story aboat a place, a people, and their food before cathing his next flight, so there’s a definite snse of urgency to Bourdain’s step.
